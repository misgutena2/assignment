Students face so many difficulties before and after joining campas. Especially after they have joined campas, their are so many things to block their path. We can mainly clasify those difficulties in to three:

  1. peer presure
  2. social media
  3. Environmental curcumstances

peer presure

If students are not strong enough to block influences coming from all directions, anyone can take them in a way that does not suit them. So they can not be able to achive what they want and what they can. Influence from outside starts even before joining campas when they are chosing their their field of study. Their parents tells them what they must study, even if they do not want to join that profesion. Others also will tells them to study another field. So even if they want to study what others told them, it is not easy to make all of them happy. Therefore what they have to do is choosing what they really want and what really makes them happy.
